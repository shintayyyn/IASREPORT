Data Protection and Privacy Laws
1. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – European Union

Establishes strict data protection rules for EU citizens and applies globally to organizations handling EU data.

Healthcare and Sensitive Data Security
2. Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) – USA

Protects sensitive patient data and requires healthcare providers to follow strict security measures.

Cybercrime and Computer Security Laws
3. Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) – USA

Criminalizes unauthorized access to computers, including hacking, fraud, and information theft.

Cybercrime and Computer Security Laws
4. Convention on Cybercrime (Budapest Convention)

An international treaty to coordinate efforts among countries to combat cybercrime and secure online activities.

Data Protection and Privacy Laws
5. Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

Canada - Regulates how organizations handle personal information in Canada, emphasizing accountability and user consent.
